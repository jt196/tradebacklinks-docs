# Dashboard Overview

The dashboard home keeps your program on track with a single glance.

![Dashboard overview](static/images/dashboard/01-dashboard.png)

- **Turn Autopilot On** – master switch to start or pause daily outreach.
- **KPI cards** – New backlinks this month, total backlinks, and average organic traffic.
- **Domain rating card** – Displays your DR plus a reminder to turn on Autopilot when no activity has occurred.
- **Potential backlinks chart** – Visualizes how many qualified opportunities are available right now.
- **Recent activity feed** – Once outreach starts, this area lists emails sent, replies received, and links won.

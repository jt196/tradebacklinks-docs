# Settings

TradeBacklinks splits workspace settings across five tabs. Use them to keep your profile, domain info, and plan up to date.

## My Details

![My Details](static/images/dashboard/07-01-settings-my-details.png)

- Update your display name and avatar. This info may appear in marketplace listings or emails.

## Domain Information

![Domain Information](static/images/dashboard/07-02-settings-domain-information.png)

- Owner name, company name, contact email, and social handles.
- Short/long descriptions plus logos are used when TradeBacklinks submits your site to directories on your behalf.

## Marketplace Listing

![Marketplace Listing](static/images/dashboard/07-03-settings-marketplace-listing.png)

- Choose whether to list your domain publicly in the marketplace.
- Specify if you require payment, the amount, and your category.

## Password

![Password](static/images/dashboard/07-04-settings-password.png)

- Update your password here. Make sure you log out of other sessions after changing it.

## Plan

![Plan](static/images/dashboard/07-05-settings-plan.png)

- Compare plans (Indie vs. Scaling) and subscribe or upgrade. Each plan lists domain outreach limits, email credits, and monitoring frequency.

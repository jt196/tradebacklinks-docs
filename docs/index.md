# Welcome to TradeBacklinks Help

TradeBacklinks automates backlink outreach for indie founders and lean SEO teams. The platform continuously discovers keyword-aligned domains, scores their health, and coordinates personalized outreach from your branded inboxes.

Use this help center to learn how to set up your account, interpret the dashboard, and resolve common issues.

## What you can do with TradeBacklinks
- Discover backlink opportunities based on your keywords and competitors.
- Automate outreach with Autopilot and your connected email domains.
- Offer compensation (swaps, guest posts, perks) inside a structured workflow.
- Monitor existing backlinks for health, spam score, and lost links.

Ready to begin? Start with [Getting Started](getting-started.md).
